The free time projects I am involved in

Next to a full time job I am involved in some activities mostly related to teaching.
I am a volunteer teacher at some Weekendschools in The Netherlands:
IMC Weekendschool and Trudo Weekendschool

Mathematics

Physics

Let children of 11 to 13 years old do 6 sets of physics experiments in 2.5 hours to let them wonder about physics.

Look at the material (in Dutch)

Making electro breadboards

One of the experiments in physics class was about electro breadboards. One child wanted to make this for himself. When asked in class who else wanted this as well, almost the entire class said "yes". In this way a new lecture was born.
In 2.5 hours they make some electronic circuits and they like it very much.

Look at the material (in Dutch)

Programming

Teaching children computational thinking and programming in Scratch. 3x 2.5 hours. Programming is done with Raspberry Pi as computer.

Look at the material (in Dutch)

Making tables for programming at the right height to also teach children how to sit at a screen

For the programming class we have a room with tables which are too high. Instructing how to sit if they use screens is then not likely to have success. Hence tables were made hanging off laths that run over the table and can be adjusted in height. And everything is set up and torn down every day, because are guests in the rooms we use. So logistics is very important.
